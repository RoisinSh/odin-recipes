Tacos

Description

Tacos have recently become one of my favourite foods because they're easy to cook and high in protein

Ingredients

  • Refried beans
  • Halloumi
  • Wraps
  • Salad

Recipe link

Or follow the steps below...

  1. Chop your halloumi into blocks and fry them with a bit of oil
  2. Heat up your refried beans
  3. Heat up your wraps
  4. When your wraps are warm, put some sauce on them if you like
  5. Then, add your salad
  6. Next, add your refried beans
  7. Finally, add your halloumi
  8. Enjoy!
